    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate is this AI automation savings calculator?
    Our calculator provides estimates based on industry averages and research on AI automation efficiency. Actual savings vary based on your specific workflows, tool choices, and implementation. Most users report achieving 60-80% of the calculated savings within 3 months of implementation.
    What tasks can AI actually automate?
    AI can automate many repetitive tasks including email writing and responses, data entry, content creation, scheduling, customer support, social media management, report generation, and research. The key is identifying tasks that follow patterns and don't require complex human judgment.
    How long does it take to see ROI from AI automation?
    Most people see positive ROI within 1-2 months. Initial setup takes 2-4 hours per automated task, but the time savings compound quickly. A task taking 5 hours weekly becomes 20 hours saved per month after a one-time 2-hour setup.
    What AI tools do you recommend for automation?
    Popular options include ChatGPT and Claude for writing tasks, Zapier and Make for workflow automation, Notion AI for documentation, and specialized tools like Jasper for marketing content. The best choice depends on your specific use case and technical comfort level.
