Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this calculator?

This calculator provides estimates based on industry averages for AI automation potential. Actual savings depend on your specific workflows, tool selection, and implementation quality. Use these numbers as a starting point for your business case.

What AI tools should I use?

Popular options include ChatGPT/Claude for content and analysis, Zapier/Make for automation, and specialized tools for your industry. Start with free tiers to test, then upgrade as you see results.

How long until I see ROI?

Most businesses see positive ROI within 1-3 months of implementing AI tools. The key is starting with high-volume, repetitive tasks where automation has the biggest impact.

Will AI replace my team?

AI augments your team rather than replacing them. Freed from repetitive tasks, your people can focus on higher-value work: strategy, creativity, relationship building, and complex problem-solving.
